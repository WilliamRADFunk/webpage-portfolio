Planetary Defense

(12/2015) -- JavaScript, ThreeJS, PHP, AJAX, & MySQL --
A 2D base defender game where player moves concentric rings as expendable barriers to interecept asteroids traveling at random vectors. Blue spheres (cosmic energy) heal barriers on contact. Scores are recorded in a database, and top 5 displayed for each new game.
